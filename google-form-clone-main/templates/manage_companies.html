How Referral Codes Work

Referral codes are used to connect your forms with third-party websites. When a user submits a form associated with a company:

  1. Our system automatically detects email fields in the submission
  2. It makes a request to https://{referral_code}.example.com/api/get-user-data with the user's email
  3. The third-party site returns additional user data
  4. This data is linked to the form response for your review

Third-party sites can also push data directly to your webhook at:
